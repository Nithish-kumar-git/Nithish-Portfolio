Featured Work

Faculty Workload Management System

A PostgreSQL analytics platform deployed for institutional workload governance Live at HITS Chennai

The Problem

The institution faced the challenge of manually allocating courses to 50+ faculty across 4 academic programs — 150+ courses, thousands of offerings — with no systematic way to measure fairness, track overload, or audit decisions.

System Architecture
PostgreSQL → SQLAlchemy ORM → FastAPI → React + TypeScript

22-table schema · 4-tier RBAC · 30+ REST endpoints

19,200+ Course offerings automated
50+ Faculty served across 4 programs
15 KPIs computed per faculty member
4-tier Access control hierarchy
Workload Classification Logic
Overloaded TCH exceeds 18-hour norm
Balanced TCH within ±2 hours of norm
Underloaded TCH significantly below norm

This wasn't a technical output — it was a business decision about fairness, requiring explicit definition of what 'fair workload' means to the institution.
